Qinbo Li

I am a research scientist at Meta. I am interested in Computer Graphics, Computer Vision, Deep Learning, and more specifically, I am interested in computational photography, image-based rendering and view synthesis.

Before I joined Meta, I received my PhD. from Texas A&M University, advised by Dr. Yoonsuck Choe.
